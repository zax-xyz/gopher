For years, the vicious murderer ninten866 has repeatedly killed Gopher, a poor innocent character from the Hundred Acre Wood, despite him not having done anything wrong. In May of 2017, during a Critical Mode Gold Crown run of Kingdom Hearts 2 Final Mix on the PS4, he claimed gopher to be his favourite character, yet shortly after brutally slaughtered him. He had an appearance of guilt and remorse, but can we really believe this facade put on?

Again on the 27th of September, he called gopher his "favourite character", and immediately decided to continue his unremorseful bloodbath.

So far, ninten has killed Gopher a staggering total of at least 20 times. This website was made in an attempt to keep Gopher remaining in our hearts, and never let him be forgotten. May he rest in peace. <3
